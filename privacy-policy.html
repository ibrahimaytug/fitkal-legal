FitKal Privacy Policy Last updated: July 17, 2026 FitKal ("we," "our," or "the app") is a fitness and nutrition coaching app that uses AI to generate personalized workout plans and meal suggestions, and includes optional social features for connecting with friends and finding local fitness meetups ("FitUps"). This Privacy Policy explains what information FitKal collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. AT A GLANCE We never sell your personal information, and we don't show ads or use ad-tracking of any kind. Health and fitness data (workouts, meals, weight, injuries) is used only to personalize your plans — never for advertising. Location is only used for features you actively engage with (FitUps, Discover Nearby), and Discover Nearby is off by default. Your exact location coordinates are never shown to other users — only approximate distance. You can export, edit, or permanently delete your data at any time from within the app. CONTENTS Information We Collect How We Use Your Information Third-Party Services Data Shared With Other Users Sharing to External Platforms Your Choices and Rights California Privacy Rights International Data Transfers Data Retention Children's Privacy Security Changes to This Policy Contact Us 1. Information We Collect Account Information Name and email address, used to create and secure your account Password (encrypted; we never see or store your password in plain text) Optional Sign in with Apple credentials Health and Fitness Information Age, height, weight, target weight, and optional body composition measurements (body fat %, waist, hip, chest) that you choose to enter Workout logs: exercises, sets, reps, weights, and duration Food logs: meals, ingredients, and estimated nutrition values, entered by text, photo, or barcode scan Step count and active calorie data, only if you grant HealthKit access Medical flags, injuries, or allergies you choose to disclose, used solely to make your workout and nutrition plans safer and more relevant Medical and injury information is never used for diagnosis, and is never shared with anyone, including other FitKal users. Photos Profile photos you upload (visible to other users per the sharing rules below) Progress photos you upload (private to you by default) Photos of meals or nutrition labels, submitted for AI analysis to estimate nutrition Optional photos taken during a workout, which you may choose to share externally (e.g., to Instagram) — see "Sharing to External Platforms" below Messages If you use FitKal's messaging feature to chat with friends, we store the content of those messages to deliver them and keep your conversation history. Messages are only visible to the participants in a conversation. Location Information FitKal requests location access only for features that need it, and only while the app is in use: To show public FitUps happening near you To power the optional "Discover Nearby" feature, which you must explicitly turn on. When enabled, your approximate location is used to show your profile to other opted-in nearby users. Your exact coordinates are never shown to other users — only your name, photo, and general distance. You can disable location access at any time in your device's Settings, and can turn off Discover Nearby at any time within the app. Usage and Device Information App interaction data (which features you use) to improve the app Device identifiers and crash logs for debugging 2. How We Use Your Information To generate personalized AI workout plans, nutrition targets, and meal suggestions To track your progress over time — weight trends, workout streaks, and achievements To enable social features you choose to use: friends, messaging, FitUps, and Discover Nearby To process in-app purchases and manage your subscription To send notifications you've enabled, such as nutrition reminders To maintain the security and reliability of the app We do not use your data for advertising. FitKal does not display third-party ads and does not use ad-tracking technology of any kind. 3. Third-Party Services FitKal relies on the following services to operate: PROVIDER PURPOSE Supabase Database and file storage for your account data, logs, and photos Groq AI processing to generate workout plans, nutrition suggestions, and analyze food photos you submit Apple Sign in with Apple, in-app purchases (FitKal never sees your payment details), and optional HealthKit integration These providers process data on our behalf under their own security commitments. We do not sell your personal information to any third party. 4. Data Shared With Other Users Certain information is visible to other FitKal users, depending on which features you choose to use: FEATURE WHAT'S VISIBLE Friends Name, profile photo, bio, and (if you allow it) whether you worked out today and your weekly count. Weight, targets, and food logs are never shared. Messaging Message content, visible only to you and the friend you're messaging FitUps Name and photo, visible to people you invite, or nearby users if the FitUp is public Discover Nearby (opt-in) First name, photo, and rough distance only. Off by default. 5. Sharing to External Platforms FitKal lets you optionally share workout summaries or photos to third-party platforms like Instagram, using your device's native share feature. Once shared, that content is governed by the receiving platform's own privacy policy and terms — FitKal has no control over it after it leaves the app. 6. Your Choices and Rights Edit or delete your profile information, photos, and logs at any time within the app Permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account Disable location, camera, notification, and HealthKit permissions anytime in your device Settings Turn off Discover Nearby anytime Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or Turkey's KVKK to access, correct, or export your data — contact us below to exercise these rights 7. California Privacy Rights If you're a California resident, you have the right to request details about the personal information we hold about you and to request its deletion. As stated above, we do not sell personal information, and have not sold personal information in the preceding 12 months. 8. International Data Transfers Our service providers may process and store data outside your country of residence, including in the United States and European Union. By using FitKal, you consent to this transfer, which is necessary to provide the service. 9. Data Retention We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is permanently removed from our systems, except where retention is required by law. 10. Children's Privacy FitKal is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us so we can delete it. 11. Security We use industry-standard measures, including encryption in transit and access controls on stored data, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. 12. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date above. 13.
